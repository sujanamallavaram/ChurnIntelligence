## 🎯 THE 3 CONTROLS (Mission Control)

### 📊 CHURN THRESHOLD (Blue Box)
**Slider: 0.10 - 0.90**
- **0.10-0.40**: Catch more customers (high recall)
- **0.50** (Default): Balanced approach
- **0.70-0.90**: Focus on critical only (high precision)

👉 **Adjust based on team size**:
- Small team → Move right (0.7-0.8)
- Large team → Move left (0.3-0.4)

---

### 👥 SEGMENT FOCUS (Orange Box)
**Select segments to analyze**
- **Enterprise**: Large accounts ($1000+ MRR)
- **Mid-Market**: Growing companies ($500-1000)
- **SMB**: Small businesses ($100-500)
- **Personal**: Individuals ($10-100)

👉 **Tip**: Check Enterprise only for VIP focus

---

### ⚡ ENGAGEMENT VELOCITY (Green Box)
**Slider: 0.1x - 3.0x**
- **<1.0x**: Engagement dropping → Risk increases
- **1.0x**: Current state
- **>1.0x**: Engagement increasing → Risk decreases

👉 **Use to**: Show ROI of engagement programs

## 🔍 WHAT CAUSES HIGH CHURN RISK?

✅ **High Risk If**:
- Haven't logged in for 30+ days
- New customer (<3 months tenure)
- Low feature adoption
- Multiple support tickets
- Personal segment
- Low engagement score

✅ **Lower Risk If**:
- Active logins (last 7 days)
- Long tenure (1+ year)
- Heavy feature user
- Few support tickets
- Enterprise segment
- High engagement score
