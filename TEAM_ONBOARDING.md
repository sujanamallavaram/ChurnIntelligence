## 📋 Quick Overview

The **Churn Intelligence Command Center** is your real-time system to:
- ✅ Identify customers at risk of churning
- ✅ Prioritize interventions based on value
- ✅ Track customer health metrics
- ✅ Simulate impact of engagement programs
- ✅ Make data-driven retention decisions

## 📚 Key Concepts

### **Churn Risk Score**
- 0-30%: Healthy (monitor only)
- 30-60%: Watch (prepare interventions)
- 60-90%: High risk (take action)
- 90%+: Critical (immediate action)

### **Priority Levels**
- **Critical**: Act within hours, executive involvement
- **High**: Act within 1-2 days, dedicated person
- **Medium**: Act within 1 week, send campaign
- **Low**: Monitor, add to nurture

### **Intervention Types**
- **Executive Outreach**: For enterprise VIPs
- **Re-engagement**: For inactive customers
- **Support**: For customers with issues
- **Training**: For new customers
- **Offer**: For price-sensitive customers

### **Segments**
- **Enterprise**: Large, strategic, high MRR
- **Mid-Market**: Growing, medium MRR
- **SMB**: Small, low-medium MRR
- **Personal**: Individuals, lowest MRR
